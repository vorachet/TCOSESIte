Emerging INCOSE Thailand

Deliver values from Systems Engineering 

The ultimate goal of this activity is to be the authorized Thai local chapter of the International Council on Systems Engineering (INCOSE) and is intended to be the focal point for the dissemination of systems engineering knowledge and promotion of systems engineering activities within Thailand. The first step to creating this association was driven by several local meetings. This website provides news and updates on our activities.
