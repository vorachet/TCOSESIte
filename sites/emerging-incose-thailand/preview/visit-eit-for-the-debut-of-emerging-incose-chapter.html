Emerging INCOSE Thailand

Meeting with Chairperson of the Computer Engineering Discipline at the Engineering Institute of Thailand (EIT) for the debut of the Emerging INCOSE Chapter

Vorachet Jaroensawas
12 July 2022,  Bangkok
 
Meeting with Prof (Adjunct). Dr. Chianchuang Kalayanamitr, Chairperson of the Computer Engineering Discipline at the Engineering Institute of Thailand (EIT - https://eit.or.th/about-eit/) to prepare for the debut of our emerging INCOSE chapter to 20+ senior committees at EIT in the next week. EIT was established in 1943 and under His Majesty the King’s Patronage. With its current members of over 40,000 engineers and corporations and the main source of engineering publications in Thailand, we are going to be part of the SE use cases in the Thailand context. We have found the best way to reach Thai engineers.
 
We will present this topic "Systems Engineering (SE), the interdisciplinary field for Thai engineers, and the development of the local INCOSE chapter under the supervision of the Engineering Institute of Thailand (EIT)  and the International Council on Systems Engineering (INCOSE)"
 
Participants 
  • Prof. Chianchuang Kalayanamitr
  • Thanaphon Chearanai
  • Vorachet Jaroensawas
